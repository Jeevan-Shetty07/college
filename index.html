Welcome to Our College

St. Philomena College, established in 1958, is one of the premier institutions of higher education in the region. We are committed to academic excellence, research, and holistic development of students.

Our lush green campus, well-equipped classrooms, dedicated faculty, and diverse courses make us the right choice for higher education.
